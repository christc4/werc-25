Interrogation of an Artist

Oh Artist,
If thou dost love me,
Take a powderkeg to all the world's art,
Light a tinderbox between each book,
Lay dynamite below the feet of saints
Then oh artist, will I love thee

Oh Artist,
Let me see your holy book,
One closer look,
And I shall disturb the dead Oh Artist, Take down that false cross and rid me of those chains, ill take your vase and splinter it into a thousand heirlooms and scatter it in the wind and deliver you to the wind and watch as i tear it to shredsw can mortal men through virture, retrace its pages Then oh artist, will I love thee

My terror will calm a crying child,
If I am delivered to St. Fawkes,
Then Oh artist,
Can thou still love me?
