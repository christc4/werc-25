A Judas Tree

If for a day joy masters me, [1]
And misery takes its leave

– Oh Judas Tree!
Oh great absantee!

Foolish was it for me to believe
To you were I to cleave

If for a day joy bests me
And my new beloved takes its leave
My misery gone,
A misery fit to grieve,

[1] - Confession, Countee Cullen
